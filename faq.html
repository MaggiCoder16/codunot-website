Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Codunot in servers?

Mention @Codunot before your message in server channels.

Can I use prefix and slash commands together?

Yes. Example: !funmode and /funmode both work.

How do I merge images?

Send 2 or more images and include one of the merge words listed in Features.

What does /transcribe support?

You can transcribe supported YouTube, Twitch, X, and Kick links up to the bot limit.

Do I need to vote for generation tools?

Yes. Generation features and interactive fun commands require an active vote unlock.

How long does one vote unlock last?

One vote unlock lasts 12 hours.

What are the main fun commands?

Use /hug, /kiss, /kick, /slap, /meme, and /bet.

Can I use Codunot in DMs?

Yes. In DMs you can type directly without mentioning the bot.

Where do I contact for premium access?

Open the Premium page and use the owner contact button.
